Subjects

Math

Key Points

  • Learn formulas daily.
  • Practice sums from each chapter.
  • Revise graphs, algebra, geometry, and statistics.
  • Write steps clearly in every answer.
  • Check calculation twice before submitting.

Important Questions and Answers

  • Q: Why is formula revision important?
    A: Because formulas are used in many chapters and help solve questions faster.
  • Q: What should you do if a question has many steps?
    A: Write each step clearly and do not skip important working.
  • Q: How can you improve in Math exams?
    A: By practicing daily, revising formulas, and solving previous papers.
  • Q: What is the best way to reduce mistakes?
    A: By checking the answer again after solving.

Science

Key Points

  • Understand concepts instead of only memorizing.
  • Revise diagrams, formulas, and definitions.
  • Learn the important reasons and effects.
  • Use simple language in theory answers.
  • Write neat diagrams wherever needed.

Important Questions and Answers

  • Q: Why is concept understanding important in Science?
    A: Because many questions are based on application and reasoning.
  • Q: Why are diagrams useful?
    A: They make answers clear and help in scoring better marks.
  • Q: What should be revised before the exam?
    A: Definitions, equations, laws, reactions, and diagrams.
  • Q: How should theory answers be written?
    A: In short, correct, and clear points.

Social Science / SST

Key Points

  • Revise dates, events, and important terms.
  • Understand causes, effects, and comparisons.
  • Learn map work carefully.
  • Use headings and points in long answers.
  • Remember important leaders, laws, and movements.

Important Questions and Answers

  • Q: Why is SST considered scoring?
    A: Because answers can be written clearly in points with correct facts.
  • Q: What is important in History answers?
    A: Dates, events, causes, and results.
  • Q: What is important in Geography answers?
    A: Maps, locations, climate, resources, and diagrams.
  • Q: How should Civics answers be written?
    A: With proper terms, examples, and simple explanation.

📖 Study Tips

  • Study daily for 1–2 hours
  • Revise regularly
  • Practice questions
  • Read weak chapters again
  • Make short notes for quick revision
